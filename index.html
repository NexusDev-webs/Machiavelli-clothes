Who We Are

The Machiavelli Story

Cairo, Egypt 🇪🇬

Machiavelli is an Egyptian streetwear brand built on one principle: clothing should say what you won't. Bold graphics, oversized silhouettes, and designs that refuse to be invisible.

The name is intentional. Niccolò Machiavelli wrote that appearances shape power. We believe what you wear is the first argument you make every day — and we make sure you win it.

Rooted in Egypt, reaching everywhere. Our drops pull from pop art, street culture, and the restless energy of a generation that takes up space without asking permission.
